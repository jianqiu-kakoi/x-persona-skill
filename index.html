Read an X account
like you'd study a person.

Point it at any X/Twitter handle and it reads the whole timeline — then returns a structured dossier of how they think, how they speak, what they engage with, and how all of it has changed over the years. Not a personality quiz. Every claim is backed by a verbatim tweet.
